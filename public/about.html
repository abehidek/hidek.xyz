About me

I'm a tecnology enthusiast, student and software developer, born in Brazil.

I started in the field about 5 years ago when I entered high school which had an informatics course that I've joined.

That course taught me basic concepts such as algorithms and markup languages like html.

However, at the end of the course, I needed to develop and show my final project, in which I've had to learn other technologies to execute my idea, that's when I really decided to continue pursuing this field.

Python and JavaScript are my main languages however, I plan to master some interesting languages like Rust, Haskell and Elixir.

I'm also, currently in love with Linux and the FOSS community, and my main distros are NixOS and Arch.

I'm constantly searching something new to do, I enjoy consuming content like articles, documentaries, books and videos, sometimes I play video games and most of the time I'm browsing the internet. I'm fascinated with history, physics, math and other fields of knowledge.

I understand both Portuguese and English, currently learning Japanese.
